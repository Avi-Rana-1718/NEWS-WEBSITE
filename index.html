Crunchyroll announces 30+ titles for winter 2021

Crunchyroll announced more than 30 series as a part of the winter anime season streaming early next year, including the highly-anticipated Dr. STONE Stone Wars, That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime S2. Other brand new series hitting Crunchyroll this coming season include the return of Re:ZERO S2, Laid-Back Camp S2, and the release of GIVEN The Movie!
